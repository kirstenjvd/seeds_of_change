A bit about us

Inspired by seeds as the foundation of goodness, we are passionate about the way our food is grown and the people who grow it. As makers of SEEDS OF CHANGE®, we want to make a difference by helping people to know where their food comes from and how it is grown.

We make deliciously good food, from the seed, to your plate.

History

Est. 1989

To preserve biodiversity and promote the use of sustainable organic agricultural practices.

SEEDS OF CHANGE® began as a small organic seed company in 1989, with a simple mission: To preserve biodiversity and promote the use of sustainable organic agricultural practices. Our company has evolved to a strong producer of organic foods. Our line of organic rice and grain blends were inspired by the notion that great taste and sustainability go hand in hand. Today, all of our products are made with the future in mind. We are inspired by seeds as the foundation of goodness, and are passionate about bringing that goodness to your plate.

Philosophy

1% of net sales to organic farming initiatives worldwide

We believe that being part of cultivating change feels good. That's why everything we do and make has the future of our world in mind. For over 20 years, the makers of SEEDS OF CHANGE® have been committed to preserving biodiversity and promoting the use of sustainable organic agricultural practices. To demonstrate our commitment, we contribute 1% of our net sales toward sustainable organic farming initiatives worldwide.

In 2016, our 1% Grant Program will donate one per cent of net sales to support community gardens across Canada this gardening season. Check out the gardens that will be working with below and join our Facebook page to receive updates of their progress throughout the year!
